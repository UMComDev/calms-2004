Glossary

Ever hear someone use a term at church that you didn't quite understand? Everyone from time-to-time has felt this way.

Even though we at UMC.org make every effort to develop stories in such a way that everyone can understand, we realize that we'll use terminology that needs some clarity.

Use our glossary to find definitions of unfamiliar terms.
