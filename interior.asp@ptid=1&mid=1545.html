Devotion: Children’s Daily Prayer for Summer
Children's Daily Prayer for Summer is a way to pray every day, all summer long, at home or in summer religious education classes. It also is ideal for schools that run year round, continue classes well into June, or begin classes in early to mid-August. At its heart is a simple order of prayer. (Cokesbury)

Book: Upper Room Children’s Bible Stories
This beautifully illustrated book of scripture stories will enrich the spiritual journey and delight the inquisitive minds of children of all ages. Its pages are brimming wth easy-to-read Bible stories, and each of the 365 stories is accompanied by its Biblical reference, so that children become increasingly familiar with scripture. Little known and fascinating facts line the margins of each page, offering information about the places, people, and customs of the time. (Upper Room)

Magazine: Devo’Zine
Devo'Zine® is a teen magazine about real life, written by real people about a real God. This unique magazine for teens is designed to link teens to their peers and to help strengthen their faith.Devo'Zine Online features sample meditations from the magazine and the DZ Question. (Upper Room)
