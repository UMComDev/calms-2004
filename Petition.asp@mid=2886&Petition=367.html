Submitted Petition Text:
Amend ¶1008.2 as follows:
  
2. Either on behalf of its total work or on behalf of one of its programs, the board may solicit and create special funds, receive gifts and bequests, hold properties and securities in trust, and administer all its financial affairs in accordance with its own rules and provisions of the Book of Discipline. Funds vested in any of the predecessor boards shall be conserved for the specific purposes for which such funds have been given.  As stipulated in the Declaration of Trust for the Methodist Building Endowment Fund of  1965, all principal and income from assets originating with the old Board of Temperance shall be restricted to work in the areas of temperance and alcohol problems.
