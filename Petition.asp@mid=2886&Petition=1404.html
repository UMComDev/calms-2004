Amend ¶205.1 as follows:
  
� 205. Definition of a Pastoral Charge-1. A pastoral charge shall consist of one or more churches that are organized under and subject to the Discipline of The United Methodist Church, with a charge conference, and to which an ordained or licensed minister is or may be duly appointed or appointable or to which a lay person is assigned as pastor in charge or co-pastor. Where co-pastors are appointed, the bishop may designate for administrative purposes one as pastor in charge.1 The lay person assigned will be trained and work in a ministry team with an ordained or licensed pastor-mentor to whom he/she is accountable; the lay person assigned may be certified as a lay minister or lay missioner.  The lay person assigned is accountable to the policies and procedures of the annual conference where assigned.
