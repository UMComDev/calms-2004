Amend �242 as follows:
  
� 242. Primary Tasks-The local church....
In carrying out ....
    In carrying out its primary task, the local church often hires staff persons.  All paid staff persons who are engaged in worship, ministry, programs, or decisions affecting the task and mission of the local church shall be persons who model themselves after the servanthood of Jesus Christ.  They shall be persons of genuine Christian character who love the Church and are committed to the oneness of the body of Christ, are morally disciplined, and shall uphold the doctrinal and ethical standards of The United Methodist Church as set forth in the Doctrinal Standards (� 103) and Social Principles. In addition, they are to be competent to perform the jobs for which they are hired. It shall be the responsibility of the committee on pastor-parish relations (or committee on staff-relations) to inform all job applicants, in writing, of The United Methodist Church’s doctrinal and ethical standards and that adherence to them is a continuing condition of employment.  All paid staff persons who are engaged in worship, ministry, programs, or decisions affecting the task and mission of the local church are to be held accountable to the same standards of character as clergypersons. It shall be the responsibility of the committee on pastor-parish relations (or committee on staff-relations) to terminate the employment of staff persons who will not uphold the doctrinal and ethical standards of The United Methodist Church, including those set forth as Chargeable Offenses in � 2702.1 and � 2702.3.
