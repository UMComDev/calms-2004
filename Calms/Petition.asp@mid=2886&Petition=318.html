New � Conference Scouting Committee
1. In each annual conference there shall be a conference scouting committee. Its purpose shall be to interpret and strengthen the local churches and districts in their use of the programs of Boy Scouts of America, Girl Scouts of the USA, Camp Fire USA, and 4-H or other appropriate civic youth serving national organizations within the central conferences  which minister to children and youth and their families. For administrative purposes, the committee shall be related to the annual conference council on ministries or equivalent structure.
2. Membership - The conference scouting committee shall be composed of the district scouting coordinator and one other representative of each district within the conference, the conference scouting coordinator and such at large members as necessary.

Nashville TN
