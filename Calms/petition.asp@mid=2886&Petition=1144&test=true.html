Submitted Petition Text:
Add new paragraph after existing �356.7 as follows:
Inactive Retired -This relationship may be granted at the time of retirement, or any time thereafter, to clergy who because of health, age, geographical location, or other personal reasons are unable or unwilling to participate in the annual conference sessions.  This relationship shall be initiated by written request to the board of ordained ministry at least 90 days prior to the annual conference session, and granted by a two-thirds vote of the clergy session upon the board’s recommendation.
Between sessions of the annual conference, this relationship may be granted by the executive committee of the board of ordained ministry, subject to the approval of the clergy session at its next session.  Inactive retired clergy shall not be required to attend the sessions of the annual conference, nor shall they be required to report by letter to the conference secretary setting forth the reason for their absence (�602.8).  They shall not have voice or vote in the sessions of the annual conference.  They shall be relieved of the commitment to participate in the life of an order (�314), though not denied participation.  They shall not be eligible to receive an appointment (�356.6).  They shall not be included in the count of clergy members for the purpose of equalizing lay and clergy membership of the annual conference (�602.4).  All other rights, privileges, and responsibilities set forth in The Discipline shall be retained by inactive retired clergy including those set forth in �356.5.  Clergy in the inactive retired relationship who desire to return to the active retired relationship shall make a written request to the board of ordained ministry at least 90 days prior to the annual conference session, giving specific reasons for the request.  Upon recommendation of the board, reinstatement to the active retired relationship may be granted by a simple majority vote of the clergy session.
