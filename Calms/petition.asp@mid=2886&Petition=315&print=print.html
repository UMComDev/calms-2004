Amend �603.9b as follows:
  
    b) The conference lay leader shall be the chairperson of the conference board of laity, or its equivalent, and shall relate to the organized lay groups in the conference such as Lay Speaking Ministries, United Methodist Men, United Methodist Women, scouting ministries, and United Methodist Youth and support their work and help them coordinate their activities.
