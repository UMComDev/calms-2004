Connectional Table Membership and Organization  
Membership of the Connectional Table shall be elected in accordance with a new paragraph, Organization of the Connectional Table.  Following the organization meetings of agencies in the fall of 2004, the Connectional Table will be convened by a Transition Team to begin its orientation, training, and the ordering of its life. The Connectional Table will begin consultation with the General Council on Finance and Administration and the General Council on Ministries to ensure the efficient and complete transition of the functions and responsibilities of each Council including transfer of property, assets, and personnel.  The Connectional Table will have the right and responsibility to implement its organizational style, develop policies and procedures, determine personnel needs, and prepare itself to fully assume the responsibilities transferred from the former General Council on Finance and Administration and General Council on Ministries.
