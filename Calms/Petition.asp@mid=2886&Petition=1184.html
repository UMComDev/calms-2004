Delete �405 and replace with the text as follows and Amend � 511.8 as follows:
  
405. Provisions for Episcopal Areas in the Jurisdictional and Central Conferences-1. In Jurisdictional Conferences, the number of Bishops assigned to the several Jurisdictions shall be as follows: the North Central Jurisdiction - 10; the Northeastern Jurisdiction - 10; the South Central Jurisdiction - 11; the Southeastern Jurisdiction - 13; and the Western Jurisdiction - 6. Any change in the number of Jurisdictional Conference bishops shall be determined on the basis of missional needs, as adopted by the General Conference on recommendation by the Interjurisdictional Committee on Episcopacy.  
2. If a jurisdiction, as a result of the provisions of this paragraph, shall have the number of bishops to which it had previously been entitled reduced, then the reduction in the number of bishops to which it is entitled shall be effective as of September 1 of the fourth calendar year after said reduction has been determined by the General Conference. If the effected jurisdictional conference has no retirement or other opening in its Episcopal offices, then its implementation shall take place September 1 following the jurisdictional conference when such opening is available.  
3. In Central Conferences, the number of bishops shall be determined on the basis of missional needs, as approved by the General Conference on recommendation of the Commission on Central Conference affairs.  
4. This legislation shall take effect immediately upon its adoption by the 2004 General Conference.
