� 263. Six  Seven special churchwide Sundays with offerings shall be celebrated in each United Methodist Church...
7. Hunger Awareness Sunday - Hunger Awareness Sunday shall be observed with an offering, preferably on the second Sunday of September.  This Sunday will remind us of the hungry Americans who send their children to school.  School may be the suppliers of the only meal these children receive that day.
A. One third of the offering is to stay local for food pantries and soup kitchens.
B. One third of the offering shall be sent to the Bread for the World to continue to educate the church on world hunger.
C. One third of the offering to The Society of St. Andrew to continue feeding the millions of hungry Americans through the Meals for Millions Program.
