Submitted Petition Text:
Add the following resolution to the Book of Resolutions:
Be it resolved, that if an annual conference board of higher education and campus ministry provides oversight of the budgetary and financial processes of a campus ministry unit, it shall provide minimum levels of funding to that campus ministry unit.
Be it further resolved, that if an annual conference requires any portion of a campus ministry unit’s budget to be raised by that unit, then the annual conference shall provide a fund raising consultant to assist.
