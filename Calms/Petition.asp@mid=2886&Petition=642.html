Add new �6 to �2545 as follows:
  
6. Process for Retiring a United Methodist Church. Should a United Methodist congregation come to a time when shifting population and demographics and/or economic conditions create circumstances that make it of questionable stewardship to maintain a viable local church organization (�� 201, 202, 203, 204), the status of United Methodist Church Retired may be requested from the annual conference after approvals have been received from a majority of the superintendents and the bishop.
The purpose of the "Retired" United Methodist Church would be to maintain a limited United Methodist presence and ministry in this setting.  The continuing ministry could consist of ministries such as:  a) funerals; b) weddings; c) homecoming celebrations; d) occasional worship service or revival; e) Sunday school class(es); f) food pantry or clothing closet; g) covenant relationship with a multi-Ethnic and\or multi-language congregation � 2551.      
The Process:
   1.     The church be merged in accordance with � 2545 with a nearby United Methodist church by a resolution stating the terms and conditions of the merger and covenant relationship. This relationship is to be reviewed annually by the board of trustees with appropriate recommendations being presented to the annual charge conference. The board of trustees may establish a property committee composed of representatives from each congregation to deal with the  physical, financial, and liability matters of the retired church.
2.    In this relationship, the merged church would consider the ministries in the retired church as an extension of its ministries with the following restrictions:
        a. No regular services of worship.
b. The church is not to continue on as an independent or autonomous church, and/or use the name Methodist, other than (Church Name) United Methodist Church, Retired.
b. The church is not to be sold to another denomination or group for the purpose of becoming a place of worship.
3.    Should the retired church come to the place where it cannot be maintained in a retired relationship as determined by the charge conference, that the appropriate items in � 2548 be carried--out by the superintendent, the District Committee on Location and Building, and Conference Board of Trustees.
