Submitted Petition Text:
Whereas, the 2004 General Conference of the United Methodist Church recognizes that the thrust of official United Methodist positions on the general distribution of material resources has strongly tended toward various expressions of state-sponsored economic egalitarianism, and
Whereas, the consequences of state-imposed redistribution(s) of wealth may be seriously diminishing resources generally available to the Church to accomplish our mission of making Disciples of Jesus Christ, therefore,
Be it resolved that the 2004 General Conference of the United Methodist Church directly calls for the General Council on Ministries and/or General Council on Finance and Administration (or successor agencies), the General Board of Church and Society and the General Board of Discipleship cooperatively to facilitate with our Bishops a general and biblically-based re-evaluation of the long-term impact of state-sponsored economic egalitarianism on the resources available to the Church.
