Submitted Petition Text:
Amend �161.C as follows:
  
C) Marriage-We affirm the sanctity of the marriage covenant that is expressed in Christian  love, mutual support, personal commitment, and shared fidelity between a man and a woman  and God who created them to become one flesh .  Sacred  marriages are the happiest, healthiest, harmonious and most enduring and prevent abuse, adultery and abandonment.  Sacred marriage is best for rearing children who are happiest, healthiest, educated and have the lowest rates of substance abuse, school dropouts, sexually transmitted diseases, out of wedlock pregnancies and births, poverty and crime. Marriage preparation, enrichment and rehabilitation be provided by every United Methodist Church minister and local church and in cooperation with neighborhood churches, synagogues, mosques and judges. Seduction and alienation of affection of marrieds is condemned.
We believe that God's blessing rests upon such marriage, whether or not there are children of the union. We reject social norms that assume different standards for women than for men in marriage including prostitution, adultery, cohabitation, and bisexuality."

Yukon OK
