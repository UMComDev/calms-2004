Submitted Petition Text:
Amend �164.A as follows:
  
    A) Basic Freedoms and Human Rights-We hold governments responsible ....Furthermore, the mistreatment or torture of persons by governments for any purpose violates Christian teaching and must be condemned and/or opposed by Christians and churches wherever and whenever it occurs. For the same reason, we oppose capital punishment and urge its elimination from all criminal codes.  Christians disagree about the acceptability of capital punishment.   The church must offer ministry to persons guilty of heinous crimes, urging repentance while offering forgiveness and healing.  So too the church must be mindful of crime’s victims, reminding them of the need to forgive no matter how difficult, while also offering sympathy and the promise that God will restore His fallen creation.
