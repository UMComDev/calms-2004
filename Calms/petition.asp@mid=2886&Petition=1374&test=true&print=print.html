Submitted Petition Text:
The current mission statement is:
            "The mission of the church is to make disciples of Jesus Christ." (2000 Book of Discipline, paragraph 120, page 87)
    The mission statement proposed has two forms:
    A)    Full text form:
"United Methodists
              Make disciples of Jesus Christ,
Mature disciples in Christian living,
Minister love and good works to all."
    B)    Memory form:
              "United Methodists Make, Mature, and Minister."
