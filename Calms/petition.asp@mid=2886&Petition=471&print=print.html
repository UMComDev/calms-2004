Submitted Petition Text:
Amend Resolution 111 paragraph 1 as follows:
  
111.  Ministries in Mental Illness
Mental illness is a group of brain disorders that cause severe disturbances of thinking, feeling, and acting. Treatment should recognize the importance of a nonstressful environment, good nutrition, and an accepting community. -as well as Treatment should also recognize the importance of medical, and psychiatric, emotional and spiritual  care, psychotherapy or professional pastoral psychotherapy--in regaining and maintaining health. Churches in every community are called to participate actively in expanding care for the mentally ill and their families and communities.
