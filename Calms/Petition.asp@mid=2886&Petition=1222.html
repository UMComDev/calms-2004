Amend � 1115.9 as follows:
  
9. Encourage ordained elders to select and train laity to distribute the consecrated Communion elements as soon as feasible to sick or homebound persons following a service of Word and Table. This distribution also may apply to laypersons who have been assigned pastoral roles in a church or in more than one church by the district superintendent. 
Encourage ordained elders to select for training by the bishop or district superintendent laity to conduct the service of Holy Communion (minim liturgy: prayer of confession; forgiveness; consecration) only for sick or homebound persons.  These laypersons will be issued an authorization certificate by the bishop and it shall be valid only in the persons' church/parish and must be renewed annually by the charge/church conference.
