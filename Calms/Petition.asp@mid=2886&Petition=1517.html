Amend �161.G as follows:
  
G) Human Sexuality-We recognize that sexuality ...
Homosexual, former homosexual, bisexual, transsexual, pedophilia, sadomasochist, bestiality, necrophilia, among other persons no less than heterosexual persons are individuals of sacred worth. All persons need the ministry and guidance of the church in their struggles for Godly human fulfillment, as well as the spiritual and emotional care of a fellowship that enables reconciling  transforming relationships with God, with others, and with self that result in joyous reconciliations. Although we do not condone the practice of homosexuality and consider this practice incompatible with Christian teaching, we affirm that God’s transforming grace is available to all through saving faith in Jesus Christ. We implore families and churches not to reject or condemn their lesbian and gay members and friends just their unhealthy and risky behavior, not former homosexuals. We commit ourselves to be in Christian ministry for and with all persons.1
