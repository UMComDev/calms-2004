Submitted Petition Text:
Whereas, Scripture is clear that God does not discriminate among God’s children and imparts God’s gifts on all of them;
Whereas, by scriptural injunction all members of the church are called to follow God’s lead and to support its mission and ministry;
Whereas, the mission and ministry of The United Methodist Church and its members is carried on in many countries organized in Central and Jurisdictional Conferences;
Whereas, the general agencies of the church provide a variety of resources to individuals, churches, and organizations across the denomination to expand and strengthen their ministries;
  Whereas, the constitution and the Discipline of The United Methodist Church grants equal rights and privileges to all members of the church and so has been ratified by rulings of the Judicial Council;
Therefore, be it resolved that all resources of the general church shall be accessible on an equal basis to all its members and organizations regardless of their resident status or country and place of service.  In those cases where, for missional reasons, certain programs need to be directed at the needs of specific constituencies and/or countries/regions, such provisions shall be made explicit in the program description and approved by the General Conference.
