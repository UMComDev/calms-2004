Submitted Petition Text:
Amend �356.1 as follows:
  
1. Mandatory Retirement-Every clergy member of an annual conference who will have attained age seventy on or before July 1 in the year in which the conference is held, requesting to continue in the itinerancy, shall automatically be retired  reviewed.43   This review will determine if the Board of Ordained Ministry shall recommend continuance or mandatory retirement to the clergy session of the annual conference. The review, which will occur in each year the request for continuance is made, shall give consideration to: effectiveness of leadership in the current appointment; health and stamina to maintain pastoral responsibilities; and, intellectual grasp and self-awareness providing reasonable self-assessment as it pertains to gifts and grace for ministry. The clergy member, desiring continuance in the itinerancy, shall submit a request to the Board of Ordained Ministry no later than six months prior to the next annual conference session. The review shall be conducted by the Board of Ordained Ministry in consultation with the cabinet and the staff parish relations committee of the local church.

This legislation shall take effect immediately upon adjournment of the 2004 General Conference.
