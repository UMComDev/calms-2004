Submitted Petition Text:
The 2004 General Conference of the United Methodist Church is requested to make a thorough review of the fiscal health of the National Council of Churches; and, if the NCC has not corrected the concerns that prompted the United Methodist Church’s 1999 action to withhold funds, steps are to be taken to work with other member denominations of the NCC to create a new agency for expression of our denomination’s historic commitment to Christian unity.
