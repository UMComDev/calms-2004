    15. To assist and advise the jurisdictions, annual conferences, districts, and local churches in all matters relating to the work of the council. These matters shall include, but shall not be limited to, business administration, investment and property management, information technology, and auditing. Matters related to resourcing the development and implementation of financial programs within the local church committee on finance shall be the responsibility of the General Board of Discipleship. The council may perform certain functions for the jurisdictions, annual conferences, districts, or local churches if the particular organization so elects and a suitable plan of operation can be determined.
The General Council on Finance and Administration and UMCom shall work together to create and manage a task force to develop and implement a Denominational Strategy for the Digital Age.
The task force will include representatives from each of the General Boards and Agencies, from annual conferences, districts, local churches, and from UMACCA, UMAC, UMACBA, NAACT, and PAUMCS.
The task force is to implement solutions as they become apparent and are within the existing budgets, guidelines, and responsibilities of the General Boards and Agencies.  The task force will report to the 2008 General Conference its findings, actions, and recommendations.
