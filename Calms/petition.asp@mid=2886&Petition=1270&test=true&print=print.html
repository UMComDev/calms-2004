Submitted Petition Text:
The Council of Bishops shall appoint a task force to clarify and conform the Discipline’s 800 and 900 paragraphs so that the roles and responsibilities are clearly reflected in all paragraphs, resulting in concise definition of the responsibilities of GCFA and GCOM, and so that the currently existing conflicts within the Discipline are resolved, as well as duplication and overlapping of responsibilities.  This task force shall submit legislation for action to the 2008 General Conference.  The Council of Bishops shall ensure that the task force has a balance of one-third clergy, one-third laywomen, one-third laymen, one-third racial ethnic.  The task force shall include youth and young adults.
Submitted ByRichard S. Parker
Babylon NY
