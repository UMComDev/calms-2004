Submitted Petition Text:
Delete current Resolution 321 and insert the following:
 WHEREAS, The United Methodist Church affirms the use of biblical language and images in worship and in our common life together, and affirms the use of language that reflects the long-standing commitment to the inclusiveness and diversity of United Methodist members and constituencies; and
  WHEREAS, humankind as male and female is created in the image of God (Genesis 1:27); God is described as a mother in Isaiah 66:13; and a mother eagle in Deuteronomy 32:11-12a; and
  WHEREAS, Jesus taught his followers what God was like using the image of a woman with a lost coin (Luke 15:8-10); and, spoke of himself as like a mother hen (Matthew 23:37), and
  WHEREAS, Jesus referred to himself when he said, “But wisdom is proved right by her actions” (Matthew 11: 18-19);
  Therefore, be it resolved, that United Methodist clergy and laity be encouraged to use diverse metaphorical images from the Bible, including masculine/feminine metaphors for God; use language for humans that reflects both male and female; use metaphors of color, darkness, ability, and age in positive rather than exclusively negative ways; and,
  Be it further resolved, that United Methodist clergy and laity be encouraged to use expansive biblical language in reference to the Trinity in liturgies; and
  Be it further resolved, that United Methodist clergy and laity be encouraged to use The Faith We Sing; and,
  Be it further resolved, that publications, audiovisual media, and other materials of The United Methodist Church should reflect the diverse biblical metaphors, as well as language that reflects the diversity and inclusiveness of humanity.
