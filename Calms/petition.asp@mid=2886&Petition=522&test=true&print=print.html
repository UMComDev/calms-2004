308.  Justice, Peace, and the Integrity of Creation
“Justice, Peace, and the Integrity of Creation” was the theme of a process initiated by the World Council of Churches, Sixth Assembly, in Vancouver, Canada, in 1983. In Canberra, Australia, in 1991, the World Council of Churches adopted this emphasis as a priority area for the council’s programs. To join the issues of peace, justice, and the well-being of creation is to create a common understanding of this interconnectedness.
