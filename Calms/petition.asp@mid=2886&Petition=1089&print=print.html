Submitted Petition Text:
In order to maintain the high level of Health Coverage presently supplied to our Pastors and also to aid the finances of our churches, beginning in the Calendar Year 2004 or the next possible Calendar Year, our Pastors contribute 10 % of that years total premium.  (If the premium would be an increase of the same amount as this year, it would total $37.69 per bi-weekly pay.)
For the year 2005 or the next possible Calendar Year, each Pastor would pay an additional 1 % of any Premium Increase exceeding 3 % of the preceding years premium. If the premium increase does not exceed 3 %, the pastor would see no increase in his contribution for that year.
Submitted ByRuth Bishop
Harrisburg PA
Epworth UMC (Church)
