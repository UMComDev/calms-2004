Submitted Petition Text:
A new, previously non-existent study committee shall be formed to analyze data in membership statistics, membership age groups by percentage, participation, giving trends, size of missionary programs, local church closings, and local church starts over the recent past.  This information shall be incorporated into a future modeling system to look at the possible impact of trends on the future.  Having assessed possible outcomes, this group shall suggest to the 2008 General Conference some meaningful strategies for “rightsizing” all aspects of the church, emphasizing fruitful ministries, organizing budgets and programs to anticipate future circumstances and provide for effective shrinking as necessary in ways that facilitate future growth from core ministries.  
    This group may hire consultants and should look for experts in the field within the membership of the United Methodist Church.  Their conclusions should be both spiritual and business minded.
Submitted ByShawn Bishop
Mansfield OH
