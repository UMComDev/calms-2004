Amend �2551.1b as follows:
  
b) In actively shared facility situations, N  no United Methodist congregation shall pay rent to another United Methodist church. However, each congregation should be expected to pay a mutually agreed share of building expenses.  A shared facility is defined as a building used as the principal place of worship by two or more congregations.  In the case of a facility available for unshared occupancy, with little or no scheduled use of the facility by the property owner, rental not to exceed fair market value may be charged to another United Methodist church.
