Amend �356 by adding a new �5 and renumbering as follows:
  
5. If the annual conference in which the conference member retires provides a post-retirement health care benefit, and if the benefit eligibility standards are based on years of service under episcopal appointment in pastoral and/or extension ministries, then all the years served under episcopal appointment must be included in determining eligibility for post-retirement health benefits regardless of the annual conference in which the years were served in the connection. Further, the cost of post-retirement benefits shall be allocated among the annual conferences in which the retired member served in the manner used by the General Board of Pensions and Health Benefits to allocate the annual conference pension obligation when a retired member has served in more than one conference.  The General Board of Pensions and Health Benefits shall coordinate the collection and disbursement of funds related to post-retirement health care benefits for all retired conference members who have served the connection in more than one conference.
