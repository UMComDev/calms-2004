Submitted Petition Text:
We as a church do not encourage, in fact, discourage the substitution of CE and BCE for BC and AD. We respect the dating system of Jewish, Oriental, Muslim, etc. cultures, and freely affirm our own tradition which has been accepted by the culture within which we live to recognize with traditional BC-AD dating, the impact of our Lord on our culture and are will continue to proclaim it in this manner which has been traditional for centuries.  United Methodists affirm the continued use of BC and AD in our literature.
  
Submitted ByG. Lynn Tunnell
Dalton GA
