Amend �218 as follows:
  
� 218. Mutual Responsibility-Faithful discipleship includes the obligation to participate in the corporate life of the congregation with fellow members of the body of Christ. A member is bound in sacred covenant to shoulder the burdens, share the risks, and celebrate the joys of fellow members. For this reason, members may not initiate litigation against other Christians; such lawsuits are forbidden by Scripture (1 Corinthians 6:1-8) and the General Rules.  A Christian is called to speak the truth in love, always ready to confront conflict in the spirit of forgiveness and reconciliation.
