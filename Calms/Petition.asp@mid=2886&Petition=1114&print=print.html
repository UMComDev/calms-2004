Submitted Petition Text:
The 2004 General Conference approves the 2005-2008 Quadrennial funding proposal for the National United Methodist Native American Center (NUMNAC) in the amount of $977,572.00.  The funds should come from the World Service Fund and other participating boards.
The 2004 General Conference endorses NUMNAC’s function and role as related to its initial goals, its proposed activities for the 2005-2008 Quadrennium, and directs that it be supported by related entities of the United Methodist Church as a Center for Native American cultural, spiritual and contemporary training for United Methodist clergy and lay persons.  It is recommended that the GCOM, the Connectional Table, or the successor body provide for the administrative and accountability relationship to NUMNAC for the general Church.

Claremont CA
