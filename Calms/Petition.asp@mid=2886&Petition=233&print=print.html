Submitted Petition Text:
Amend �409.1a as follows:
  
1. Mandatory Retirement-a) A bishop shall be retired on August 31 next following the regular session of the jurisdictional conference if the bishop’s sixty-sixth birthday has been reached on or before July 1 of the year in which the jurisdictional conference is held.6  A bishop having reached his or her  sixty-sixth birthday on or before July 1 of the year in which the jurisdictional conference  is held, requesting to continue in the episcopacy, shall automatically be reviewed.  This review will determine if the Jurisdictional Committee on Episcopacy shall recommend continuance or mandatory retirement to the Jurisdictional Conference.  The review, which will occur in each year the request for continuance is made, shall give consideration to:  effectiveness of leadership in the current appointment; health and stamina to maintain Episcopal responsibilities; and intellectual grasp and self-awareness providing reasonable self-assessment as it pertains to gifts and grace for ministry.  The bishop, desiring continuance in the Episcopal office, shall submit a request to the Conference Committee on the Episcopacy no later than six months prior to the next Jurisdictional Conference session when action is required.  The review shall be conducted by the Jurisdictional Committee on the Episcopacy in consultation with the Conference Committee on Episcopacy of the Annual Conference being served by the bishop.
  
This shall take effect immediately upon adjournment of the 2004 General Conference.
