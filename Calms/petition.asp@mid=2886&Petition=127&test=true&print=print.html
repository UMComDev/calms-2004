Submitted Petition Text:
Add new resolution to the Book of Resolutions as follows:

The General Commission on the Status and Role of Women and the General Commission on Religion and Race shall spend the next quadrennium planning and creating a single Council on Inclusiveness (“Council” because it is to be an administrative rather than a program agency).  Their work will continue to include monitoring, advocacy, and education around issues related to gender and race discrimination, and they will add attention to other aspects of compliance with inclusiveness as described in �138. Called to Inclusiveness and �4. Inclusiveness of the Church.  The new Council structure shall be submitted for approval to the 2008 General Conference.
While it was important to begin our path toward an inclusive church through attention to racism and sexism, these two boxes now limit our vision.  Many instances of discrimination include both gender and race, so these two groups should be united.  Moreover, there are many persons who encounter other kinds of discrimination not currently covered by the current structure, such as persons with physical disabilities.  The new configuration would allow us to monitor our hospitality to all people in pursuit of truly open hearts, open minds, open doors.
Submitted ByGail Murphy-Geiss
General Commission on the Status and Role of Women (General Agency)
