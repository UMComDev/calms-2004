Submitted Petition Text:
Amend Comprehensive Protection Plan Section 5.04c(1)i as follows:
  
Any persons already receiving a disability benefit on the Effective Date shall continue to receive the amount previously awarded to them, which was based on 40% of the Denominational Average Compensation for the Plan Year in effect on the date of the first payment (as adjusted for the increased provided in Section 5.04c(3) below).  Any person already receiving a disability benefit on the Effective Date shall receive the greater of an amount equal to 70% of the annualized Plan Compensation for that Participate for the Plan Year in effect on the date of the first payment or the current benefit.
