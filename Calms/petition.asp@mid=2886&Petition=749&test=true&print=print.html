Submitted Petition Text:
Whereas the General Board of Ministries is still located in New York City even though the 1992 General Conference voted to move it:
Whereas many persons believe it is the staff of this agency which is the principal reason why the move away from New York City is needed;
Be It Therefore Resolved by the 2004 General Conference that when the General Board of Ministries moves to a new location the number of employees who move with it shall not exceed 5 percent of the existing staff.
