Submitted Petition Text:
The Minnesota Annual Conference petitions the General Conference to establish a Denominational Taskforce that will:
  1)    Research the effectiveness of apportionments as a funding method for mission and administration.  
    2)    Survey funding systems used by all other denominations and networks of churches
3)    Examine seriously the biblical pattern of tithing as a model for denominational funding and partnership with annual conferences and local church  
4)    Report to the 2008 General Conference their findings about apportionments and other systems of funding, and
5)    Propose to the 2008 General Conference any needed legislative changes that will implement an optimum funding system to promote A) health in the relationships that exist with local churches, annual conferences and general church, and B) the expansion of Christianity through The United Methodist Church.
