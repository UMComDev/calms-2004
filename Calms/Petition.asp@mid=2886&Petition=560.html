Submitted Petition Text:
Amend �349 by adding a new subparagraph 3 and renumbering the existing subparagraphs as follows:
  
3. All full-connection clergy serving under full-time pastoral appointment shall be required to take renewal leave once every six years. Local pastors under full-time appointment may also request renewal leave, provided that all other edurequirements have been met. Such leaves are available only after having held full-time appointment for six years. Renewal leaves are to be for a period of not more than thirty days and may be for physical or spiritual renewal. The conference board of ordained ministry shall provide to clergy a listing of possible renewal programs, or the clergy member may design her or his own program, subject to approval by the district superintendent. All requests for renewal leave must be made in writing to the pastor-parish relations committee and the district superintendent at least six months prior to the time the leave is to begin. The request shall include all necessary details, such as pulpit supply, finances, etc.
