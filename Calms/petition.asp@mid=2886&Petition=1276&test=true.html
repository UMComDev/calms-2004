Submitted Petition Text:
The United Methodist Church shall establish a study on “Learning to be Peacemakers in a World at War.”  
   
This study for the next quadrennium shall include the following:
  
1.    We, the UMC, accept the challenge of Jesus of Nazareth “to learn the things that make for peace.”  We seek peace within our own lives, the life of our families, our churches, communities, country, and with others through out the world.

2.    That the study be prepared by the GCOM and the Just Peace committee and be funded within their budget.

3.    The study should include:

a. Insights of past Nobel Peace prizewinners for bringing peace to our world.
  b. Exploring alternatives to war that have been used successfully in India, the U.S.,
South Africa, etc.  Mandela and Bishop Tutu would be great resource-people.
  c. Reaching out to other denominations, world religions, and peace organizations so that they might become involved with us in this study and in any subsequent action.

d. Encouraging our President, Congress, and other government leaders to establish a Secretary of Peace in the President’s Cabinet, a Department of Peace, and a national Peace Academy.

e. A curriculum for adults, youth, and children that will include TV and video programs to help us as a people to become as skilled in peacemaking as we are in war-making and violence.

Submitted ByM. Wesley Arms
Gig Harbor WA
