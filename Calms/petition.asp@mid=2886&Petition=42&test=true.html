  10.  In light of our constitutional commitment to Ecumenical Relations (division one - General, paragraph 5, Article V.) the commission should give consideration in setting standards for clergy support for those ministers who have had their orders recognized for service in The United Methodist Church (par. 337.2) and whose years of service include ministry exercised in other denominations, especially those coming from the churches of the Commission on Pan-Methodist Cooperation and Union and other member churches of the Churches Uniting In Christ.
