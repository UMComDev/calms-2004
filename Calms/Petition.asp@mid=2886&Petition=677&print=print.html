Submitted Petition Text:
Amend �315.2 as follows:
  
2. Service Requirement: Each candidate for probationary membership and commissioning shall have had a minimum of two years in an approved service setting as determined by the district committee on ordained ministry as a condition for probationary membership and commissioning. This required service may be concurrent with academic study, provided there is adequate mentoring and supervision in the theology and practice of servant leadership. The District Committee on Ordained Ministry, in consultation with the District Superintendent, shall approve the service setting for candidates not under appointment, and for all candidates determine that this requirement has been fulfilled.   A service setting shall be in a position approved by the bishop and cabinet in the conference where the candidacy relationship is held, even if the service setting is outside the bounds of the annual conference.
