� 331. 4 Self-Care and Local Ministry Insight -a)  The appointed pastor within the first year of a new appointment, and every subsequent year meet periodically with peers in ministry including United Methodist pastors, and optionally pastors of other Christian denominations.  Laity of other parishes or denominations who are identified by the pastor as having growing and deepening faith may also be invited into this network.  These people shall provide insight, be supportive and encouraging, act as accountability partners, and assist with discernment.  The pastor shall verify to the district superintendent that this network exists.
b) The pastor shall informally identify for him- or her-self laity within the local church who are of growing and deepening faith and who can provide mature insight into the circumstances, history and issues related to the local context.  These people may informally help the pastor navigate her or his charge by their contribution and support.  This network may be formal or informal, but verification of such people shall be made to the district superintendent who will approve the ultimate formation, membership and role of said network.
