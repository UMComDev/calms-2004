Submitted Petition Text:
Amend � 165C by deleting the current paragraph and replacing as follows:
  
We believe that war is tragic and yet sometimes necessary and should be undertaken only after all efforts have been made to resolve the conflict peacefully.  Even then, war can only be declared by a sovereign nation.  The intent of those waging war must be to promote the good and to avoid evil with the hope that the prosperity of peace can be brought to the enemy.  The craving to hurt people, the cruel thirst for revenge, the unappeased and unrelenting spirit, the savageness of fighting on, the lust to dominate, and suchlike - all these are rightly condemned in war.
We recognize the need for military chaplains who can provide spiritual nourishment for others, and we affirm a chaplain's role as a non-combatant.  Additionally, we pray for our men and women in uniform with the hope that their participation will be deterrence for war or the reason for victory.
