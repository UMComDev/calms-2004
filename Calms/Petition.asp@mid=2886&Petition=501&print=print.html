Submitted Petition Text:
WHEREAS, the doctrine of the Trinity is one of the central affirmations and tenets of the Christian faith, and
  
WHEREAS, the Church scripturally and traditionally has always understood that the name for the triune God is some form of “Father, Son, and Holy Spirit,”
  
Be It Therefore Resolved that The United Methodist Church through the action of the 2004 General Conference affirms its belief in that triune God--Father, Son, and Holy Spirit--and asks its pastors to baptize and its bishops to ordain only in that name.
