Submitted Petition Text:
The Book of Discipline and The Book of Resolutions will be made freely available in their entirety to the church and the world.  These documents will be provided in the most effective medium economically commensurate with their import.
At a minimum, free access is to be provided to both the Discipline and the Resolutions in their entirety via the UMC Web site.  These documents are to be presented in a searchable format.  Footnotes and references to other documents (such as Judicial Council decisions) and intra-textual cross-references shall be hyperlinked.  Errata and corrections shall be updated in a timely manner.  The availability of these documents shall be widely publicized.
The United Methodist Church should also strongly consider further measures for freely providing clergy with this information, for example, making these documents in their entirety available in a format downloadable to PDAs (e.g., a Palm) and providing clergy with a CD-ROM of these documents along with the above-mentioned research tools.
