Amend � 325 by adding a new paragraph 4 and renumbering the existing paragraphs as follows:
  
4.  "Any Bishop, Elder, Deacon, or any person of position or title connected to the United Methodist Church, who speaks at any kind of official function where people are listening and/or is covered by the Media (Newspapers, T.V. etc.). And when the people who are listening know and understand that this person is a member of, or connected to the United Methodist Church.    
If that person speaking, speaks in any way that is detriment to The United Methodist Church beliefs about Jesus the Christ.  
If the statements in any way take away from the status in which we hold Jesus through our stated beliefs; then, that person speaking, representing in any way, the United Methodist Church; will turn in their resignation to their position in the Church."
