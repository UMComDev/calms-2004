Submitted Petition Text:
WHEREAS, the General Board of Pension and Health Benefits has proposed through its Benefits 2004 Task Force a major change in the current pension plan of the denomination; and
WHEREAS, one component of the new proposal is a Defined Benefit Plan; and
WHEREAS, Defined Benefit Plans are passé in the current U. S. business climate because of the potential risks of future unfunded liabilities; and
WHEREAS, the two per cent (2%) cap on increases in the Pre-82 Past Service Rate places an extra burden on conferences with an unfunded liability and unduly punishes their retirees with most or all of their service pre-82; and
WHEREAS, significant changes to correct anticipated or actual problems with the current plan have been made recently (i.e., market rate on annuities instead of the previous 8 % guarantee and the discontinuance of the Diversified Investment Fund, etc.); and,
WHEREAS, the aforementioned changes have not been in force long enough to determine their impact;
Therefore be it resolved, the 2004 General Conference refer the proposed new plan to a 2008 Benefits Task Force to be chosen by the General Board of Pension and Health Benefits in accordance with Paragraph 1502.4d of the Book of Discipline.
Submitted ByEd Tomlinson
Norcross GA
North Georgia Annual Conference Board of Pension and Health Benefits Executive Committee
