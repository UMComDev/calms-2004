Amend �2713.3a as follows:

3. a) The trial for a clergy member or a local pastor shall be convened as provided in �  2709 with the pool of thirty-five or more persons to consist of a minimum of 15 laity from the annual conference board of laity or the lay delegates to general and jurisdictional conference and a minimum of 20 clergy in full connection. If there are not enough persons in appropriate categories in an annual conference to complete the pool, additional persons may be appointed from other annual conferences. All clergy appointments to the pool shall be made by the district superintendents and lay appointments shall be by the drawing of lots from those defined above as eligible persons.  Special consideration should be given so that the pool includes persons representative of racial, ethnic, and gender diversity.
