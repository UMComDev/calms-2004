Amend �806.9 as follows:
  
9. It shall be responsible for ensuring that no board, agency, committee, commission, or council shall give United Methodist funds to any gay caucus or group, or otherwise use such funds to promote the acceptance of homosexuality. The council shall have the right to stop such expenditures.18  This restriction shall not limit the Church’s ministry in response to the HIV epidemic. Neither shall it limit funding to groups, workshops, resources or events that encourage biblical and theological dialogue  within our denomination  about homosexuality  or United Methodist positions such as support of civil and human rights for lesbian and gay people, ministries with lesbian and gay people,  and the belief in the sacred worth of homosexual persons.  This restriction does not block funding of resources that contain historical, scientifically, sociologically, medically, or psychologically accurate information about homosexuality.  Neither does it restrict the use of local, district or conference United Methodist funds.
