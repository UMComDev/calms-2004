Paragraph 2517.  District Parsonages, Other District Property, and Boards of Trustees
  
    1.  Each district shall provide adequate housing for its superintendent. If the district conference determines that a district parsonage should be purchased or leased, it shall refer the matter to the district parsonage committee, the district board of church location and building, and the district council on finance and administration, which groups shall consult with each other and report their recommendations to the district conference. The district conference may adopt a resolution for such purchase or lease and shall include a plan for financing in the resolution. The annual conference may provide for alternative procedures for districts to acquire or lease suitable housing for superintendents to fulfill their responsibility to provide adequate housing.
