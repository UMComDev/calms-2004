Submitted Petition Text:
AMEND � 243 by adding a new subsection 4 as follows:
4.  All decisions and/or resolutions concerning the ongoing life of the local church made by the church council, committee on pastor-parish relations, board of trustees, committee on finance, committee on lay leadership, alternative structure, the church treasurer, lay leader, and lay member(s) to annual conference shall be consistent with the Doctrinal Standards (� 103) and Social Principles.  A United Methodist member may request a ruling from the Bishop on a decision or resolution  made by the local church which is believed to be in violation of the Doctrinal Standards (� 103) or Social Principles.  The request for ruling shall be made by submitting the request in writing to the Bishop, with a copy to the pastor and chair of the church council.  The request for ruling should cite the decision made or resolution adopted by the local church and the specific Doctrinal Standard or Social Principle which is believed to have been violated.  The Bishop must rule and respond in writing to the member, the pastor, and to the chair of the church council within 30 days.  All rulings by the Bishop shall have automatic review by the Judicial Council at its next regularly scheduled session.
