Submitted Petition Text:
Whereas, it is becoming more and more obvious that there are many individuals in the United States who are antagonistic to Christianity and are doing everything they can to drive Christ not only from any reference in Government but also from Society itself; and
Whereas, this is well represented by the effort of the New York City School Board to have the Jewish Menorah and the Moslem Crescent present in schools in November and December of 2003 while prohibiting the Cross;
Therefore, be it resolved that the 2004 General Conference of the United Methodist Church urges all United Methodists to speak out clearly when efforts are made to remove Christ from anything in society as our failure to clearly speak out will result in our becoming either a Godless society or a pagan one.
Submitted ByHoward Lydick
Richardson TX
