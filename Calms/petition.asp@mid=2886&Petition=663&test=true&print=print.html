Amend �225.4 as follows:
  
4. It shall be the duty of the pastor, the parent(s), guardian(s), sponsor(s), or godparent(s), the officers and teachers of the church school, and all members of the congregation to provide training for the children of the Church throughout their childhood that will lead to a personal commitment to Jesus Christ as Lord and Savior, to an understanding of the Christian faith, and to an appreciation of the privileges and obligations of baptism and membership. The pastor shall, building on the preparation that youth have received throughout their childhood, organize them into classes for professing membership  and confirmation (� 5) . This instruction shall be based on materials that the youth have already used and on other resources that present the mission, history, and doctrines and beliefs of produced by The United Methodist Church for the purpose of confirmation preparation . When the youth so prepared shall give evidence of their own Christian faith and purpose, and understanding of the privileges and obligations of professing membership, they may be received as full  professing members.
