Submitted Petition Text:
Amend � 403 by adding a new paragraph to be numbered 403.1 as follows:
  
The United Methodist Church understands clearly that we are not an hierarchical church. Our bishops are given duties which do not permit assignment, as active or retired, to positions or jobs not included specifically in their disciplinary responsibilities. Specifically, they may not serve as a chief operating officer; secretary or representative bishop of the Council of Bishops or the United Methodist Church. The bishops cannot establish an Episcopal office or headquarters apart from the area which they serve or where they retire.
(This legislation takes effect immediately upon adjournment of the General Conference.)
