Amend � 1806.21 as follows:
  
21. The General Commission on Communication shall be charged with planning and implementation of the official United Methodist presence on and use of the Internet, the World Wide Web, or other computer services that can connect United Methodist conferences, agencies, and local churches with one another and with the larger world.  Each elder, deacon, local pastor, and diaconal minister in good standing with the United Methodist Church shall be provided, through the UMC.org website, a web-based e-mail account with an e-mail address that will stay with that person throughout her/his ministry.
