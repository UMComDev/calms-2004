Submitted Petition Text:
Amend the Preface to the Social Principles as follows:
  
PREFACE
  The United Methodist Church ...
The Social Principles are a prayerful and thoughtful effort on the part of the General Conference to speak to the human issues in the contemporary world from a sound biblical and theological foundation as historically demonstrated in United Methodist traditions. They are a call to faithfulness and are intended to be instructive and persuasive in the best of the prophetic spirit; however, they are not church law. The Social Principles are a call to all members of The United Methodist Church to a prayerful, studied dialogue of faith and practice. (See � 509.)  Neither the Social Principles nor the Social Creed shall be regarded as a part of the law of The United Methodist Church nor interpreted as having the force of church law.
