133.  Native American Young Adults in Mission

Whereas, opportunities ...
Therefore, be it resolved, that the Mission Personnel Resources Program Department, World and National Divisions,  Area be directed to assign Native American young adults in “Native American Nations and Tribes” within the boundaries of the United States and in overseas assignments.
