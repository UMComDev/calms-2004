Submitted Petition Text:
Resolved that the 2004 General Conference authorizes, requests and instructs the General Board of Church and Society pro-actively to engage with governmental proposals and/or programs like the "Healthy Marriage and Family Formation Initiative", with particular emphasis and attention to the appropriate roles of Church and State regarding healthy marriages and families and the 2004 General Conference hereby instructs the General Board of Church and Society to report, with recommendations, to the 2008 General Conference on practical ways and means that the Church, in general, and United Methodists, in particular, may properly cooperate with civil authorities for the purpose of engendering healthier marriages and families.
Submitted ByPat A. Pruitt
Midland TX
