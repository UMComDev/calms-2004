Submitted Petition Text:
The names of all bishops ordained or consecrated by bishops of The United Methodist Church or predecessor churches shall be printed in The Book of Discipline, regardless of the church in which they served.
Examples of bishops who served outside of The United Methodist Church or its predecessor churches are Bishops William H. Miles and Richard H. Vanderhorst, founding bishops of the Christian Methodist Episcopal Church, who were consecrated by Bishops Robert Paine and Holland McTyeire of the Methodist Episcopal Church South in 1870.
