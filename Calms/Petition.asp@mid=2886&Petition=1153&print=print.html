Amend �� 2539 and 2540 by inserting new Section 4 in each paragraph and renumbering the existing material as follows:
  
4.  In order to assure continuity of a United Methodist presence and ministry in a given location or area: a) All United Methodist churches and United Methodist ministry sites and properties which are to be closed or abandoned must be considered to be given first to a United Methodist church or United Methodist ministry site in need of a church building or property in that immediate area.  b) If there is no United Methodist church or ministry in need of a building or property the building or property should be considered to be given to a Pan-Methodist church or ministry site in need of a building or property. c) If there is neither a United Methodist nor Pan-Methodist church or ministry in need of property and the building or property is to be sold, the funds from the sale shall be used exclusively for the capital or program cost of United Methodist or ecumenical ministry in which the United Methodist Church is significantly involved so that there continues to be a United Methodist presence and ministry in that community or area.
