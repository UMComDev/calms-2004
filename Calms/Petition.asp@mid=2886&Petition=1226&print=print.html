2.  When real estate is being donated to an unincorporated local church only as a gift to be sold for the purpose of providing assets or income for the church to use in its ministry and mission, the board of trustees of the unincorporated local church may petition the district superintendent to declare such property as gift property, a contribution of assets for mission and ministry. The receipt or sale of gift property may be transacted by the Board of Trustees of the local church without charge conference action provided that a statement from the district superintendent determining the said property as gift property shall be entered into the charge conference minutes at its next meeting. Written consent to such action shall be given by both the pastor and the district superintendent.

Gift property shall not be subject to the requirements of entering the trust clause into the deed or instrument of conveyance.
