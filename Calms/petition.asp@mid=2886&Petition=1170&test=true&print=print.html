Delete �162.H and replace as follows:
  
H) Equal Rights Regardless of Sexual Behavior--Basic human rights with their responsibilities and civil liberties with their obligations are due all persons including heterosexuals, homosexuals, former homosexuals, bisexuals, transsexuals, pedophiles, sadomasochists, bestiality, and necrophilia. We are committed to supporting those rights such as "innocent until proven guilty", and responsibilities such as avoiding high risk behaviors, and liberties such as "free speech" for all persons equally. Special privileges or protections should not be provided for any individual or group based solely on a sexual behavior or preference. Moreover, we support efforts to stop violence against all persons without any sexual discrimination. The Good News of eternal life is offered to all through a saving faith in Jesus Christ.
