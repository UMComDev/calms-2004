Submitted Petition Text:
    WHEREAS, the 2000 General Conference adopted the "National Comprehensive Plan for Town and Country Ministry of The United Methodist Church: Born Again in Every Place"; and
WHEREAS, the foundational study also found that "the laity made two overwhelming appeals to the connectional system: 1) 'provide us with training and resources appropriate to town and country situations'; and 2) 'listen to us about matters affecting church life and potential in our communities'"; and
WHEREAS, the town and country economies create the need for affordable materials available from United Methodist agencies;
        THEREFORE BE IT RESOLVED that The United Methodist Publishing House and the General Board of Discipleship and other program agencies develop educational resources for town and country ministry downloadable or reproducible for local church use.
