Submitted Petition Text:
Methodists Associated to Represent the Cause of Hispanic Americans supports the recommendation of the General Council on Ministries for the Holistic Strategy on Africa and the Holistic Strategy on Latin America and the Caribbean special programs.
We further support the development of a missional fund of $5 million dollars to provide additional funding for mission and ministries in this region to be distributed as follows: $4 million for the Holistic Strategy on Africa and $1 million dollars for the Holistic Strategy on Latin America and the Caribbean.
