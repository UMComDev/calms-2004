Submitted Petition Text:
The 2004 General Conference hereby directs the General Council on Finance and Administration to present a proposed budget for the 2004-2008 quadrennium which does not require the general program agencies to make any and/or further reductions in their unrestricted funds, and further directs GCFA to immediately amend the proposed budget previously submitted in the event that same does not comply with these terms; and
    That GCFA shall immediately develop, or enable the development, of planned giving programs for each of the individual general program agencies, each of which shall be designed to increase each agency’s unrestricted endowment funds in order to support additional ministries in their areas of responsibility.
