Submitted Petition Text:
WHEREAS, the National Committee on Ministries with Deaf, Late-Deafened, Hard of Hearing, and Deaf-Blind People has reached 28 annual conferences, eight seminaries, and six general boards and agencies with awareness events in the 2001-2004 quadrennium;
  
WHEREAS, the work of the National Committee has provided seed money for projects in all United States jurisdictions of The United Methodist Church and mission work in six nations outside the United States; and
  
WHEREAS, these successes of the National Committee during its first quadrennium of existence are only a beginning for additional work in furthering awareness and resourcing ministries with deaf, late-deafened, hard of hearing, and deaf-blind people (see full report elsewhere in ADCA);
  
Therefore, be it resolved, that the 2004 General Conference of The United Methodist Church authorize an allocation of $350,000 in the 2005-2008 quadrennium for the National Committee on Ministries with Deaf, Late-Deafened, Hard of Hearing, and Deaf-Blind People, thereby continuing its vital work with God’s people, in the United States and other countries, through awareness events, grants, publications, and other God-guided ways.
