Submitted Petition Text:
WHEREAS there are many varieties of human sexual activities. Among them are heterosexual, homosexual, bisexual, group, sado-masochism (with pain), fetishism (such as black leather), pedophilia (with children), bestiality (with animals), incest (with relatives), prostitution (for money), fornication (pre-marital), adultery (not spouse), rape (non-consensual), and necrophilia (with the dead). Ted Bundy killed 28 women. Jeffry Dalmer killed 17 males and ate some. Both claimed before dying that they had an irresistible compulsion.
WHEREAS some humans at the same time participate in more than one sexual activity as for example bisexuality.
WHEREAS some humans change their sexual activities. In the USA about one-third of men who have done same-sex activities eventually become exclusively heterosexual. This is prevalent among male prisoners. About one-half of females who have done same-sex activities eventually become exclusively heterosexual. About 1% of heterosexuals change to exclusively homosexuality before dying.
WHEREAS Masters and Johnson have reported 71.6 % conversion from homosexuality to heterosexuality. (Homosexuality in Perspective). Dr. Gerhard van den Aardweg reported a 65 % success rate. (Richard A. Cohen, Perpetuating Homosexual Myths) Dr. Joseph Nicolosi, Harvard, has confirmed such conversions through his therapy. (Reparative Therapy of Male Homosexuality)
WHEREAS the USA Center for Disease Control reports annually that homosexual men infect about 40,000 men with HIV and about 4,000 die of AIDS.
WHEREAS obituaries in USA homosexual publications report the male AND female average age at death is about 45 years old. This compares with about 75 for the total USA population.
WHEREAS heterosexual marriage is the healthiest, happiest, and most enduring of all sexual relationships. It is also the best for rearing children including adoptees who are MOST healthy, happy, educated, employed and LEAST likely to drop out of school, to use illegal drugs, have unwed pregnancies and births, have an abortion, get welfare, and commit crimes.
THEREFORE:
1. The teaching of The United Methodist Church by ministers, bishops, seminaries, colleges, general boards and all other agencies is to be sexual celibacy before and exclusive sexual faithfulness during a heterosexual marriage between one man and one woman.
2. Counseling about sexual activities shall include the facts above and that change in sexual activities is possible when highly motivated.
3. Offer the Gospel of eternal life by God's grace through saving faith in Jesus the Christ that transforms the heart, soul, mind and strength.
