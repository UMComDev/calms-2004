Amend � 1602 as follows:
  
� 1602. Membership-1. The board of The United Methodist Publishing House, hereinafter called the board, shall consist of up to forty-three thirty-three members as follows:
    a) Episcopal members-Up to three Two bishops selected  nominated by the Council of Bishops, one of which shall be from Africa, and elected by the General Conference.
b) Jurisdictional and Central Conference members- Thirty members elected by the jurisdictional conferences based on the following formula: North Central - 6, Northeastern - 6, South Central - 7, Southeastern - 9, and Western - 2; provided that no jurisdiction shall be represented by fewer than two members. Twenty members shall be elected by the jurisdictional and central conferences.  These members shall be allocated among our global church on a ratio based on the membership established by the Secretary of the General Conference in determining the distribution of the members of the General Conference.  The electing conferences shall consider persons with special skills in publishing, education and significant business operations.  It is recommended that those elected include women, young adults, and racial and ethnic groups.
c) Additional members-Up to ten additional members may be elected by the board, with consideration given to representation of women and racial and ethnic groups not otherwise elected by the jurisdictions, and to special knowledge or background in publishing, marketing, graphic arts manufacturing, production of audiovisuals or electronic media, finance, retail stores, accounting, or other business fields. It is recommended that persons elected by each jurisdiction be inclusive of women and ethnic groups - Asian Americans, African Americans, Hispanic Americans, Native Americans, and Pacific Islanders.
d) Central Conference Members - One central conference member elected by the Council of Bishops.
 e) At least two  one person s who are  is a young adult s at the time of election shall be elected each quadrennium.
