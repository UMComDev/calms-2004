Amend �162.H as follows:
  
H) Equal Rights Regardless of Sexual Orientation or Gender Identity - Certain basic human rights and civil liberties are due all persons. We are committed to supporting those rights and liberties for homosexual  all persons whatever their sexual orientation or gender identity. We see a clear issue of simple justice in protecting their rightful claims where they have shared material resources, pensions, guardian relationships, mutual powers of attorney, and other such lawful claims typically attendant to contractual relationships that involve shared contributions, responsibilities, and liabilities, and equal protection before the law. In addition, we oppose heterosexism in all its forms.  Moreover, we support efforts to stop violence and other forms of coercion against gays and lesbians  all persons whatever their sexual orientation or gender identity. We also commit ourselves to social witness against the coercion and marginalization of former homosexuals.
