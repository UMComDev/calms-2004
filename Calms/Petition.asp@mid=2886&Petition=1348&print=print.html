Submitted Petition Text:
Amend �246.13 by adding a second paragraph as follows:
  
13. As soon as ... Payment in full of these apportionments by local churches is the first benevolent responsibility of the church (� 812).  
Starting in 2007, churches shall be given the opportunity to indicate at their annual Charge/Church Conference how they will financially support the mission and ministry of the Church.  Each church will have two options.  The first choice is to pay to a general fund that shall be administered by the General Council on Finance and Administration to the various ministries and funds of the denomination.  The second choice is for local churches to adjust the amounts given to each of the general agencies supported by World Service and the six other funds.  Churches may opt for the second choice so long as the total of their giving for that year is not less than the total amount apportioned to them by the apportionment formula.  Should the church give less than that amount, all funds revert to the general fund administered by the General Council on Finance and Administration.
