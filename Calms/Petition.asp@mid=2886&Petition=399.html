Housing Expenses-Upon receipt of a budget from the episcopal residence committee, the general Council on Finance and Administration shall provide funds from the Episcopal Fund to share in the costs of providing an episcopal residence or housing allowance, the amount of such funds to be set by the council in accordance with a policy approved by the General Conference on recommendation of the council.
