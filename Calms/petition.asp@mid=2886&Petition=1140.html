Amend �359.1a as follows:
  
a) Supervision - In the course of the ordinary fulfillment of the superintending role, the bishop or district superintendent may receive or initiate complaints about the performance or character of a clergyperson.  A complaint is a written and signed statement claiming misconduct or unsatisfactory performance of ministerial duties.  The person filing the complaint and the clergyperson shall be informed by the district superintendent or bishop of the process for filing the complaint and its purpose.  Once a complaint is filed, a Victim Impact Statement will be available to any Annual Conference body which reviews the offender, such as the Board of Ordained Ministry.
