 Professional Development
Continual Growth Encouraged
As a professional association of Christian educators, CEF encourages its members to continually grow in their skills and abilities to lead the church in ministry, as well as in their own personal spiritual growth. A wide variety of opportunities are suggested to CEF members to make an intentional effort to do this for themselves. Churches with staff members working in the field of Christian education are also encouraged to extend their support of professional development as important for their Christian educator.
 CEF Conference 2008
CEF & Preaching from the Center National Conference
Join us for the CEF national conference in beautiful Albuquerque, New Mexico, Oct. 24-27, 2008! Our theme is "Blessed to Be a Blessing: Christian Education for the 7th Generation." For the first time, CEF will join with the Preaching from the Center conference, making this a tremendous event for all educators, pastors, preachers, and volunteers. We will gather to reflect on the ways we can think long-term as Christian educators and to be a blessing to one another and to all of creation.
