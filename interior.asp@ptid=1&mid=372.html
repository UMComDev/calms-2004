Get involved in your own back yard! The UMC.org Volunteer Opportunities board allows you to search for global volunteer opportunities offered in local church settings. The success of Volunteer Opportunities depends largely on you, the user, submitting new mission opportunities and participating. We encourage you to not only submit items that are one time events, like painting a house, but to submit regularly scheduled, long-term volunteer opportunities.


NOTE: Please be aware that when you post a volunteer opportunity it will also be listed on our Needs registry in the UMC.org Community. When your volunteer opportunity expires, it will be archived on both UMC.org and the UMC.org Community.


If you're interested in participating in a mission opportunity beyond the local church, please visit the United Methodist Volunteers in Mission Web site.
