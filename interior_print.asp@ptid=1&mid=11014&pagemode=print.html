Web Ministry Overview: A Self-Directed Exploration
formerly Web Ministry 101

Web Ministry Overview introduces the basic steps you can take to get your local church on the Internet and establish a dynamic Web ministry. It is presented in four 30-minute online, self-directed (on-demand) training sessions.

To register for Web Ministry Overview, click here. Under the "Web Ministry" category, select "Web Ministry Overview: A Self-Directed Exploration." You will be asked to complete a brief online registration form. Web Ministry Overview is offered free by United Methodist Communications.
