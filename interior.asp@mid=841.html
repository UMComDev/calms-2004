Chaplain Speaks About Experiences in "Nameless Desert"
When there is a war, God doesn't sit on the sidelines, says Chaplain Daniel Nigolian. God sends in the chaplains.

From Jan. 19 to May 21, Lt. Colonel Nigolian was in "an undisclosed location, in a nameless desert, someplace on God’s Earth." As a Wing Chaplain of the 78th Air Base Wing, Robbins Air Force Base, Warner Robbins, Ga., he was answering his country’s call to service.

Students Pack for the Road Less Traveled
Reading, Pa. --They made it clear in worship services, plenary sessions, workshops, small groups, resolution discussions, even in their music. United Methodist college students know they are today's, not tomorrow's, church. They see themselves as leaders, traveling down an unusual path.

Nearly 300 students from colleges and universities around the country focused on the theme, "The Road Less Traveled" at the annual Student Forum of the United Methodist Student Movement (UMSM), at United Methodist-related Albright College, May 22-25. They represented 55 of the 64 U.S. annual (regional) conferences; international students represented eight countries.
