Graphics Library | Organizational Graphics

To save a copy of a United Methodist organizational graphic please click on the desired resolution. Once the image has loaded, right click on the image and select "Save Image As..." (May also be "Save Picture As..." or "Download Image to Disk...")

Images are available in one of the three following formats:

  • GIF (Graphic Interchange Format): Most compatible format for Web use. Transparent background.
  • JPEG (Joint Photographic Experts Group): Works well with Web photographs and naturalistic
    artwork. Transparent background.
  • TIFF (Tag Image File Format): Not for web use, excellent for print and for converting to other formats. Solid white background.
