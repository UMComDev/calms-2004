Sermon Hints:

Talk about Discipleship as a way of life, what we do everyday that brings us closer to Christ and identifies us as his followers.

Exodus 33:12-23 (verses 12-13 and 16) - Emphasize the fact that God does know us by name and is ready to teach us God’s ways. Talk about the fact that God promises to go with us and that because God’s Presence is with us people will know that god is pleased with us and that is what distinguishes us from other people on the face of the earth.

Psalm 99 (UMH 819) – Verses 8-9 talk about calling on God and how God answered them and that they kept God’s statutes and decrees. God has given us an example to follow in Jesus Christ, because we believe in him, we are forgiven, and called to follow his precepts.

I Thessalonians 1:1-10 - Talk about the work that was produced by faith, and labor prompted by love, and endurances inspired by the hope in our Lord. Remind the people that God has chosen them and that they are to imitators of the disciples and the Lord, in spite of suffering, and that their faith in God can become known everywhere. Be doers of the word not just hearers.

Matthew 22:15-22- As disciples of Jesus Christ we are called to pledge to God our obedience and commitment. This is our primary responsibility as followers of The Way.

However as citizens in a nation we are called to pay money due for the services and benefits that we receive. Discipleship is doing as Jesus would do in every circumstance of our lives.

Micah 6:8 – What is required of those who want to live a life of Discipleship?

Matthew 28:19-20 – The Great Commission – to go into all the world and make disciples – but first we must be disciples.

Matthew 25:31-46 – How do we live out our discipleship. If we do these things to others we are doing it to Christ. If we do not...

John 13:1-17 – An example of discipleship is being a servant. Are we willing to humble ourselves? Pick up our towels and serve as Jesus did?
