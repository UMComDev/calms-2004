Music Reviews: Print Syndication

Twice a month, UMC.org, the official online ministry of The United Methodist Church, publishes two critical reviews of a recently released albums of various genres. What makes Music Reviews unique is that it pays special attention to the spiritual elements of each compilation.

UMC.org feels a responsibility to only highlight albums that we believe are worth your time to listen to, and spend your money on.

In this day and age where the price of developing and delivering quality content is mounting, UMC.org is offering Music Reviews to Christian-related organizations free for print syndication.

As a  subscriber, you will receive the latest review and appropriate artwork by e-mail the first and third weeks of each month. To subscribe, simply insert your e-mail address in the field below and follow the instructions.
