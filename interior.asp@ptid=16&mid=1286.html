Beliefs & Practices

United Methodists affirm the faith shared by all Christians, with particular emphasis on God's grace and on Christian living, putting faith and love into action.

Explore how United Methodists understand deep questions of faith and Christian practice.

Share

Ask The UMC-FAQs

Do United Methodists believe "once saved, always saved?"

Only God can initiate salvation. But only by our ongoing, living relationship with God through faith can God's salvation be fully realized in our lives.

More

Ask The UMC-FAQs

What is the UM position on abortion?

United Methodists affirm the sanctity of all life. We trust women to make decisions that honor life with the support of the church and medical providers.
