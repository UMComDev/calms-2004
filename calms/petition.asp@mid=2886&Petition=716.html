Submitted Petition Text:
Resolved:  That the Pacific Northwest Annual Conference petition the 2004 General Conference of The United Methodist Church to support the historic United Methodist practice of "open table" at Holy Communion which is consistent with our denominational emphasis of Open Hearts, Open Minds, Open Doors.  This would mean that we will continue to include every person who responds to the invitation, not excluding those who have not been baptized or who are distinguishable by any other sign, practice, or characteristic.
Submitted ByWesley E. Stanton
Pacific Northwest Annual Conference (Annual Conference)
