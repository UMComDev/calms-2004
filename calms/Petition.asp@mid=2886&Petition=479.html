Submitted Petition Text:
Revise third paragraph of section III of Resolution 246 as follows:
  
III. Public Funding Issues
By almost any standard of judgment....  
The wide disparities among public schools....  
We acknowledge the debate over whether public funds might appropriately be used to remedy the lingering effects of racial injustice in our nation’s educational system. Families with limited resources, trapped by middle-class flight in failing urban schools, have little choice but to send their children to private or parochial schools. Without some public support, such a choice may be impossible for them.
We do not purport to resolve our differences over this issue....
