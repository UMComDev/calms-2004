  3.    In the case of a deacon in full connection who has retired from the annual conference and is not serving an appointment in a local church, that deacon’s voting rights at the annual conference will be limited to voting for delegates to General and Jurisdictional Conference, matters of ordination, character, and conference relations of clergy, and Constitutional matters.  The retired clergy, however, will retain voice on all matters where clergy in full connection have a vote.
