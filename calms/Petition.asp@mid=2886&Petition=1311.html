Amend ¶ 164.A as follows:
  
While our allegiance to God takes precedence over our allegiance to any state, we acknowledge the vital function of government as a principal vehicle for the ordering of society. Because we know ourselves to be responsible to God for social and political life, we declare the following relative to governments:
A) Basic Freedoms and Human Rights-We hold governments responsible for the protection of the rights of the people to free and fair elections and to the freedoms of speech, religion, assembly, communications media, and petition for redress of grievances without fear of reprisal; to the right to privacy; and to the guarantee of the rights  for the promotion of the general welfare, including viable, fair and lawful means to obtain to  adequate food, clothing, shelter, education, and health care.
