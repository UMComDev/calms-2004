Submitted Petition Text:
Each annual conference will require each pastor to offer to their parishioners an opportunity to study Understanding John Wesley’s A Plain Account of Christian Perfection: A Perfect Love by Dr. Steven W. Manskar and Methodist Doctrine: The Essentials by Dr. Ted A. Campbell at least annually during the 2004-2008 quadrennium.
The above two resources will also be a mandatory part of the curriculum for all new adult membership classes and/or spiritual formation experiences prior to adults committing to their membership vows within the United Methodist Church throughout the same quadrennium.
