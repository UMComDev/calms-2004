Amend ¶303.1 as follows:
  
� 303. Purpose of Ordination-1. Ordination to this ministry is a gift from God to the church. In ordination, the church affirms and continues the apostolic teaching and ministry through persons empowered by the Holy Spirit. As such, those who are ordained make a commitment to conscious living of the whole gospel and to the proclamation of that gospel to the end that the world may be saved.
