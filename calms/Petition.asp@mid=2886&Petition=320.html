Each district shall have a district scouting committee.
1. Each district of an annual conference shall elect or appoint a district scouting coordinator. This person may be male or female, preferably with knowledge and experience in one or more of the civic youth serving agencies (Boy Scouts of America, Girl Scouts of the USA, Camp Fire USA, 4-H or appropriate national organizations within the central conferences) and willing to represent all agencies equally. A district scouting coordinator may appoint assistants. The district scouting coordinator shall be related to the district council on ministries or equivalent organization.  
2. Each district in an annual conference shall have a district scouting committee. Local church scouting coordinators would represent their churches on this committee, under the leadership of the district scouting coordinator. Additional members at large as necessary may be on the committee.
