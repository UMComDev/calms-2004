Submitted Petition Text:
Whereas, there is an urgent need for pension support in the Central Conferences.
  
Therefore, be it resolved that emergency grants for pension support be made available to retired clergy and surviving spouses of clergy in African Central Conferences and other Central Conferences identified as having extreme need, to be distributed by and in amounts to be determined by the General Board of Global Ministries on an as-needed basis.  It is the intention of this petition that these grants will eventually be subsumed by the pension programs established by the Central Conference Pension Initiative.  
  
The Initiative was established by the 2000 General Conference, will make an interim report at the 2004 General Conference and is being developed by the General Boards of Global Ministries and Pensions and Health Benefits, the United Methodist Publishing House and United Methodist Communications.  The funds for emergency grants will be raised by the General Agencies participating in the Central Conference Pension Initiative.

2.  Terms of the emergency pension support grants:
  
a.  Grants described in this resolution will begin in 2005 and extend until a Central Conference Pension Plan begins.  It is understood that during the emergency grant period, the Central Conference Pension Initiative will undertake a number of test pension projects.
  
b.  The emergency grant period will be administered by the General Board Of Global Ministries and governed by a committee including two Central Conference Bishops who will not be from Conferences where plan tests are being conducted.  The committee will have some overlapping membership with the Central Conference Pension Initiative Task Force.
  
c.  Lump sum payments will be made to Annual Conferences based upon the number of claimants requiring assistance.  Standards for eligibility and emergency grant rates will take into account economic circumstances in each Annual Conference, current Annual Conference pension rules and available funds.  Funds will be paid quarterly or annually.
