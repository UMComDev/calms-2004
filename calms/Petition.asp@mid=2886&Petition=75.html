Submitted Petition Text:
Amend ¶356.1 as follows:
  
    1. Mandatory Retirement-Every clergy member of an annual conference who will have attained age seventy on or before July 1 in the year in which the conference is held shall automatically be retired .43 be subject to a retirement review proceeding.
    The retirement review proceeding shall be administered by a Retirement Review Board comprised of the clergy member’s District Superintendent, current Staff Parish Relations Chair, and at least one member of the Board of Ordained Ministry.
   
The purpose of the retirement review proceeding is to receive and process information relative to making appropriate retirement decisions for those individuals who are now seventy years old.
  
Aside from those clergy members who have elected to retire, this proceeding provides an opportunity for clergy members who wish to continue in active ministry of the Church and to express their willingness to accept appointments.
  
a)    Notice of the time and place of the proceeding shall be sent to all clergy members who are eligible to retire.
      b)    The request to postpone retirement should be stated in writing and submitted to the bishop, cabinet, and board of ordained ministry no later than January 31st of the year in which the clergy member will reach age seventy.
      c)    The request should contain specific information relative to whether the individual is mentally and physically vital and able to perform the ministerial work.
      d)    The Retirement Review Board shall seek evidence and receive testimony from others as to whether the person is mentally and physically vital and enthusiastic about the role of minister.
    e)    If the Retirement Review Board after examining the evidence agrees that the person is capable and able to continue in the active ministry of the Church, then the Board may grant a one year suspension of the retirement requirement provided however, that the person who obtains the waiver must follow the same procedure as outlined above at the end of the year if he or she desires to remain in the active ministry continues.
