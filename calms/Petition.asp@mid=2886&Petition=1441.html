Amend �337.2 as follows:
  
2. Elders or Ordained Clergy from Other Denominations-On recommendation of the board of ordained ministry, the clergy members in full connection may approve annually clergy in good standing in other Christian denominations to serve appointments or ecumenical ministries within the bounds of the annual conference while retaining their denominational affiliation; provided they present suitable credentials, give assurance of their Christian faith and experience, and other qualifications  release required psychological reports, criminal background and credit checks, and reports of sexual misconduct and/or child abuse. They shall submit, on a form provided by the conference board of ordained ministry:
(1) a notarized statement detailing any written accusations or convictions for felony, misdemeanor, incident of sexual misconduct and/or child abuse; or  
(2) a notarized statement certifying that this candidate has neither been accused in writing nor convicted of a felony, misdemeanor, incident of sexual misconduct and/or child abuse.
They shall give evidence of their agreement with and willingness to support and maintain United Methodist doctrine, discipline, and polity.
