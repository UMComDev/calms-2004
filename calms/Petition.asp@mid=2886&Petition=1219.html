Submitted Petition Text:
General Conference calls on the Council of Bishops to study the boundaries of the jurisdictional conferences by appointing a Committee on Jurisdictional Boundaries amenable to the Council to work throughout the 2005-2008 quadrennium. The Council of Bishops shall present a report on the study to the General Conference in 2008. As it chooses, the Council may enlist the aid of Church agencies to carry on the study and to draft legislation to present to General Conference in 2008 if changes in jurisdictional boundaries are deemed to be desirable.
The Committee on Jurisdictional Boundaries should be composed of at least the following persons:  one bishop from each jurisdiction, one additional cleric from each jurisdiction, and two laics from each jurisdiction, one man and one woman. The Council of Bishops shall choose a bishop, who may be a retired member of the Council, to chair the committee.
  
A budget of $33,350 per year for the years 2005, 2006, and 2007 will be provided from the Episcopal Fund for the work of the committee.
