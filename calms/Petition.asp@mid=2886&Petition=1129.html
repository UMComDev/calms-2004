Submitted Petition Text:
    THEREFORE BE IT RESOLVED that the General Conference directs The United Methodist Publishing House to:
      1.    Edit and publish the General Rules in a form that can be appropriately used for private or public study, meditation, and worship; and to
2.    Make this form available to all local churches as an insert that may be affixed to an inner cover of The United Methodist Hymnal; and to
    3.    Make this form available at no cost to local churches; and to
    4.    Include this form in subsequent shipments or editions of new hymnals; and
    5.    To develop an inexpensive study guide that may be used in studying the rules and their applicability in contemporary contexts.
