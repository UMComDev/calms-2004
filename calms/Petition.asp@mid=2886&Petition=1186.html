Amend ¶806.9 as follows:
  
9. It shall be responsible for ensuring that no board, agency, committee, commission, or council shall give United Methodist funds directly or indirectly, wholly or in part to any gay caucus or group, pedophilia, incest, prostitution, sado-masochism, bestiality, or necrophilia or otherwise use such funds to promote the acceptance of homosexuality  non-heterosexuality. The council shall have the right to stop such expenditures.18  This restriction shall not limit the Church’s ministry in response to the HIV epidemic and other diseases.
