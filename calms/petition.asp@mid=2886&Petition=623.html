Submitted Petition Text:
Amend ¶1205.4a as follows:
  
    a) Voting Members-Four representatives selected from each annual conference council on youth ministry.... The youth chairpersons from each jurisdiction shall be in addition to the three youth and one adult from their annual conferences. Voting members shall also include one youth from each of the established racial and ethnic national caucuses (Black Methodists for Church Renewal, Metodistas Associados Representando la Causa de Hispano-Americanos, National Federation of Asian American United Methodists, Native American International Caucus, Pacific Islander National Caucus of United Methodists) to total five racial-ethnic minority youth.
