Report No. 9
SUNDAYS WITH GENERAL CHURCH OFFERINGS

  
The General Council on Finance and Administration, in consultation with the General Council on Ministries and the Council of Bishops, makes recommendations to the General Conference regarding any offerings to be received in connection with special days observed on a churchwide basis. All such recommendations are subject to the approval of the General Conference.

SPECIAL SUNDAY OFFERINGS

Special Sunday Offerings for FY 2002 totaled $6,254,885. This total represents a 6.00% decrease or $399,078 less than FY 2001. Mid-year Special Sunday Offerings for FY 2003 are 6.8% less than FY 2002 totals at the same point in the fiscal year.  

CHURCH PARTICIPATION FY 2002
  
[PLEASE REFER TO ADCA p. 531]
  
  
Eighty percent (80%) of the annual conferences responded to this survey. The following information was taken from survey results:

  • Church Participation ranged from a high of 39% for One Great Hour of Sharing to a low of 20% for United Methodist Student Day;
      Church Participation for the six Special Sundays for all jurisdictions combined, averaged only 26%;
    • There were only two instances in which church participation was 50% or more; and

    • The Western jurisdiction had the highest average of church participation for all Special Sundays combined at 39.5%.

    Directives

    The following directives shall apply to each of the six general Church special Sunday offerings:
      
    1.    Promotion of all authorized general Church special Sunday offerings shall be by the GCOC in consultation with the administering agencies. Expenses of promotion for each offering shall be a charge against receipts in an amount determined in the manner described in section 7, Report No. 11.
      
    2.    Receipts from all authorized general Church special Sunday offerings shall be remitted promptly by the local church treasurer to the annual conference treasurer, who shall remit monthly to the treasurer of the GCFA.  Local churches shall report the amount of the offerings in the manner indicated on the Local Church Report to the Annual Conference. In the case of Native American Ministries and Peace with Justice Sunday offerings, the annual conference treasurer shall divide the receipts as specified in the referenced paragraphs of The Book of Discipline.
