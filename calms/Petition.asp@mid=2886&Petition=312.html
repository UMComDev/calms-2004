4. Men’s Ministry Sunday -Men’s Ministry Sunday shall be observed annually on a Sunday designated by the local congregation. The day is to celebrate the men’s ministry within and beyond the local church. This includes: organized (chartered or unchartered) units of United Methodist Men; male Emmaus reunion communities; work teams; prayer groups; Bible studies; study and enrichment groups; and other places and organizations where men of The United Methodist Church join together for fellowship, nurture, spiritual development, witness, and outreach. Resources for this observance shall be provided by the General Commission on United Methodist Men.
