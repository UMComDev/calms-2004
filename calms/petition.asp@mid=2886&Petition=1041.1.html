Submitted Petition Text:
  WHEREAS, There is need for a quantifiable definition of town and country areas for use in research and analysis in order to identify mission and ministry needs and opportunities for town and country churches and communities; and
WHEREAS, previous definitions have not been targeted specifically enough to match cultural and socio-economic understandings of town and country settings; and
WHEREAS, the Implementation Team for the National Comprehensive Plan for Town and Country Ministry has proposed the use of a targeted, quantifiable definition of town and country locations for use in research and analysis,  
Therefore, be it resolved that, for purposes of research and analysis in service of program development, the general boards and agencies of The United Methodist Church use as a definition of town and country:  Town & Country areas include “rural” areas of population density of 200 persons per square mile or less, and towns, villages, and incorporated areas of 10,000 or less.
