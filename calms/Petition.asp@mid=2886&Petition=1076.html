Submitted Petition Text:
Amend �162.K as follows:
  
K) Adoption-We recognize that C children are a gift from God to be welcomed and received. We recognize that some circumstances of birth make the rearing of a child difficult. We affirm and support the birth parent(s) whose choice it is to allow the child to be adopted. We recognize the agony, strength, and courage of the birth parent(s) who choose(s) in hope, love, and prayer to offer the child for adoption. In addition, we also recognize the agony, strength, and courage of some couples who choose in hope, love, and prayer to be able to care for a child that they are unable to give birth to themselves. We affirm and support the adoptive parent(s) desire to raise a child adopted into their family as they would a biological child. affirm the receiving parent(s) desiring an adopted child. When circumstances warrant adoption, we support the use of proper legal procedures. When appropriate and possible, we encourage open adoptions so that a child may know all information and people related to them, both medically and relationally. We support and encourage greater awareness and education to take place in order to promote the need to adopt a wide variety of children into a wide variety of families through foster care, international adoption, and domestic adoption.  We commend the birth family, the adoptive family, parent(s), the receiving parent(s), and the child to the care of the Church, that grief might be shared, joy might be celebrated, and the child might be nurtured in a community of Christian love.
