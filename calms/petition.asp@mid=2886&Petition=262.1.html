Submitted Petition Text:
Be it therefore resolved, that United Methodist Communications shall identify potential or augment existing partnerships among United Methodists in Africa, Europe, Asia and the United States,  
  
Further, be it resolved, that United Methodist Communications will collaborate with central conference bishops, communicators and other leaders to determine the nature of each partnership and choose appropriate, specific, supportive communications linkages and training,
  
Further, be it resolved, that United Methodist Communications will draw on the strengths of each partner, informed by mutual experiences, to use appropriate technology to give voice to those who otherwise might not be heard,  
  
Further, be it resolved, that United Methodist Communications will produce culture-appropriate messages to share stories that will arise out of each partnership and that reveal the church’s compassionate voice as it seeks to express the good news of God’s love for the world,
  
Finally, be it resolved, that the amount of $2.3 million (in actual receipts) shall be included in the budgets of the general church to be apportioned to the annual conferences within the jurisdictions to provide financial support for a central conference communications initiative.  Fund receipts for the initiative shall be disbursed to and administered by United Methodist Communications.
