� 264. Three special Sundays without churchwide offering shall be approved by General Conference - upon recommendation of the General Council on Ministries after consultation with the Council of Bishops - shall approve three special Sundays without offering.. The program functions assigned to the general agencies are carried out by the respective agencies through normal  regular programmatic channels. "Special Sundays" designations are not needed for these program functions to be implemented.
1. Heritage Sunday-Heritage Sunday shall be observed on April 23, the day in 1968 when The United Methodist Church was created by the union of The Evangelical United Brethren Church and The Methodist Church, or the Sunday immediately following that date. It falls  occurs during Eastertide, the season in which we remember the Resurrection and triumph of our Lord. Heritage Sunday calls the Church to remember the past by committing itself to the  God's continuing call of God. The observance of Heritage Sunday shall be under the general supervision of the General Commission on Archives and History. Any general agency of the Church which  that desires to recommend a Heritage Sunday theme for a given year for this Sunday may do so one year prior to  before the observance for which the recommendation is made. This r  Recommendations shall  is to be made to the General Commission on Archives and History, and the decision of the annual theme of this Sunday shall be made by the voting members of the General Commission on Archives and History  voting members of the commission shall determine the annual theme.
2. Laity Sunday-Laity Sunday shall be observed annually, preferably on the third Sunday in October. Laity Sunday calls the Church to celebrate the ministry of all lay Christians, as the Holy Spirit empowers their lives are empowered for ministry by the Holy Spirit. The observance of Laity Sunday shall be under the general supervision of the General Board of Discipleship. Any general agency of the Church that desires to recommend a Laity Day theme for a given year for this Sunday may do so two years prior to  before the observance for which the recommendation is made. This r  Recommendations shall  is to be made to the General Board of Discipleship, and the decision of the annual theme of this Sunday shall be made by the voting members of the General Board of Discipleship  board shall determine the annual theme.
3. Organ and Tissue Donor Sunday-Organ and Tissue Donor Sunday shall be observed annually, preferably on the second Sunday in November since  because the date is close to Thanksgiving and is viewed as a time to come together around the issues of life and Thanksgiving. Congregations are encouraged to support Organ and Tissue Donor Sunday by including the topic in their worship services. Worship resource materials are available at all of the not-for-profit donor programs in the United States.
