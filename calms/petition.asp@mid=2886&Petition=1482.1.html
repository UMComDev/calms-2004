Submitted Petition Text:
There are retired pastors in Cuba that served in the Cuba Annual Conference before 1968 while it was part of the Florida Episcopal Area.  These pastors participated in the pension plan available at the time for the Methodist Church.  Therefore these persons are entitled to benefits from the United Methodist Board of Pensions.  Nevertheless, due to the US embargo against Cuba these Cuban pastors have not received all the benefits that they are entitled to in a timely manner.
The General Conference of the United Methodist Church instructs the Board of Pensions to provide all the benefits that these Cuban pastors are entitled to.  The Board of Pension shall seek a US treasury license or remit the funds through the General Board of Global Ministries using their license to remit funds to Cuba.
