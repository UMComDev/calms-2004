Amend �2548.6 as follows:
  
6. (a) Any gift, legacy, devise, annuity, or other benefit ....
(b) In order to clarify the wishes of those that may have prepared a gift, legacy, devise, annuity, or other benefit at some time in advance of a discontinuation or abandonment of a local church for the secular court and/or probate laws in place in any state, county, or other governmental unit, a discontinued or abandoned local church may be incorporated or reincorporated as an entity of the annual conference within whose jurisdiction the said discontinued or abandoned church was located for the express purposes of receiving such gift, legacy, devise, annuity, or other benefit.  Such newly formed corporation shall exist for the sole purposes of being the legal entity to dispose of its existing property, and any other property to which its predecessor corporation or other entity may become entitled, as the rightful successor to any gift and/or bequest or other transfer made to such discontinued church.  All proceeds of property sales and all gifts and/or bequests received by a corporation established under the provisions of this paragraph shall remain the property of the annual conference.  Unless prohibited by local law, the Conference Board of Trustees shall serve as trustees of such a corporation.
