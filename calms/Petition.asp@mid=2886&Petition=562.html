Submitted Petition Text:
The General Conference directs the General Board of Pension and Health Benefits to create a Compassionate Pension Program by combining contributions of the clergy and lay employees, contributions of the General Board of Pension and Health Benefits, and annual conferences and assisting retired or disabled clergy and lay employee families with a life-time pension program for children disabled prior to age 22 to the 2008 General Conference, including consideration of prior years of service of those clergy and lay employees when there was no compassionate pension program, and establishing the minimum number of years required for vesting.
