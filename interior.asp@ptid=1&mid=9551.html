UMC.org Profiles Podcast

Podcasts are audio broadcasts created and stored digitally on the Internet. Instead of being broadcast over the airwaves once and lost, like with traditional radio, podcasts were created to be stored and played at the user's convenience. Think of them as radio shows waiting to be downloaded.

This is a free service of UMC.org. We ask that you not modify the podcasts in any way. UMC.org reserves the right to modify or terminate the feeds at any time without notice.

How do I subscribe to the UMC.org Profiles Podcast?

iTunes Method (recommended)

  1. Make sure you have the latest version of iTunes (a free download).
  2. Once you have the new iTunes software installed, you can download the free podcast automatically without having to install the podcast software.
  3. Go under the “Advanced” tab and click on the “Subscribe to Podcast” button as shown below.
  4. Enter the following URL into the box: http://archives.umc.org/rss/RSS_umcPodcasts1_15.xml and click "OK"

Now iTunes will automatically check and download the latest UMC.org Profiles podcast when it's available.

Everything Else

  1. Download the Juice podcast receiver, or some other podcast player.
  2. Setup the software to check for new podcasts. Open Juice and click the "Status" tab. Enter the Profiles feed: http://archives.umc.org/rss/RSS_umcPodcasts1_15.xml - into the "Add feed manually" window, then click "Add"
  3. Check the feed. Select "UMC.org Profiles Podcast" and then click the "Check selected feed" button. The software should download the latest feed straight to your computer.
  4. Set it up to automatically download the new Profiles. Click the scheduler tab. Check the box "check for new podcasts automatically." Set the time you would like the computer to check for new podcasts.
