Beyond the Roll Book

By Cyrs Zinkiewicz*

Churches are called to make disciples, and the Sunday school provides a great opportunity to do so. But most churches could use a boost to reach out beyond the children, youth, and adults who already are on the roll book. Beyond the Roll Book provides a very doable two-hour workshop for key persons in the church to see the potential and develop an action plan that fits their context.

Grounded in a Bible study, the group will also hear from Bishop Scott Jones (Kansas Area) in two DVD video segments, presenting helpful insights into our cultural context and the mission of the Sunday school from its beginnings. The 32-page resource, written by Diana L. Hynson, Director of Learning and Teaching Ministries at the General Board of Discipleship, also provides an effective process and the handouts needed to make leading the group easy and the workshop productive. For groups that want to take more time, the resource provides expansion activities and alternative schedule options. The DVD-ROM includes not only the video portions, but also the handouts so they can be customized if desired.

*Zinkiewicz, editor of youth publications at United Methodist Publishing House, will also be a workshop leader at the 2006 CEF Conference.
