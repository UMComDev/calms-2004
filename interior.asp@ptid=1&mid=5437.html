What is Welcoming?

Welcoming is how we show kindness, acceptance, and hospitality to everyone - guests and members alike. People catch glimpses of Christ through their encounters with us. In a nation that's becoming more unchurched all the time, we need to ensure that seekers encounter in us a Christ that they want to know better.

Looking within you place yourself in the footsteps of a visitor to find welcoming barriers. Resources such as Mystery Guest Audit and Walk-thru Worksheet can help you discover and address all kinds of physical and perception barriers.

Stepping into your community can be as simple as a door hanger campaign, or a radio PSA. How about classified ads (yes, Igniting Ministry offers graphics for classified ads). If you would like to (yourself, or in partnership with other local churches) have a television presence, our Matching Grants program might be able to help you double your budget!

Model Welcomers

St. John's United Methodist Church
Rock Hill, South Carolina

Membership: 2,500
Attendance: 477

Welcoming newcomers is the focus at St.John's. Not only do members treat newcomers special, but they also focus on accessibility. They've added a new elevator and hundreds of new signs. They spent one year developing a new website, and have also focused on their television outreach.

St. John's impressed us with their tireless effort to be accessible, involved in the community, and their one-on-one focus for members to seek out and partner with newcomers with a great sense of hospitality.
