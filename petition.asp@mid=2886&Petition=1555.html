Submitted Petition Text:
Amend paragraph 5 of the proposed Clergy Retirement Security Program from the General Board of Pension and Health Benefits as follows:
  
To continue the present feature of the Comprehensive Protection Plan (CPP) which allows for the "pension build-up" to a minister's pension account when a clergyperson's compensation, as defined by the provisions of the Ministerial Pension Plan and/or the Comprehensive Protection Plan, is less than the Denominational Average Compensation.  This build-up will be an amount which is equal to 3% of the difference between the clergyperson's actual compensation and the Denominational Average Compensation in effect for the same plan year.  Eligibility rules for this optional benefit would be the same as in the present plan provisions which include: being under an Episcopal appointment, being mandatory for all eligible participants, and having compensation which is at least 60% of the lesser of the Denominational Average Compensation (DAC) or the Conference Average Compensation (CAC).
  
Due to the legal and technical language and the complexity of the proposed Clergy Retirement Security Program, this petition requests that the General Board of Pension and Health Benefits submit a list of additional paragraphs in both the Discipline and the proposed CRSP information which would be affected as a result of the passage of this petition as well as proposed changes to the language of the paragraphs which would enable this legislation if passed.
Submitted ByMiriam C. Hagan
Statesboro GA
