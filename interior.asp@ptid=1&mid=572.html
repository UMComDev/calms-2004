What Is Web Ministry?
United Methodist Communications has launched a new, free, self-directed Web ministry online course. In "What Is Web Ministry?" you can discover the biblical and theological basis for Web ministry and begin to develop a dynamic Web presence for your church.
[More]
Web Ministry Overview: A Self-Directed Exploration (formerly Web Ministry 101)
United Methodist Communications offers Web Ministry Overview, a four-part, self-directed online course designed to empower every local United Methodist church to effectively use the Internet as a ministry tool.
