Abingdon Press announces Live B.I.G.

Do you dream of children who are excited to come to Sunday school? who come to know and love Jesus and want to live out their belief in God? who develop faith that will last a lifetime?

Abingdon Press introduces Live B.I.G., a DVD-based Sunday school resource designed to excite and enrich your children's spiritual lives by bringing their everyday real world experience into Sunday school.

Available June 2006, the Fall 2006 curriculum will offer Sunday school teachers a comprehensive, multimedia experience to share with their students ages 3-12. Click MORE to find out how you can bring Live B.I.G. to your congregation.

Young People needed to address General Conference 2008
What concerns young people? What issues do young people confront? What dreams do young people have for the future? What word do they have to share with the church today?

A young person or a team of young people will tackle these questions when they address the 2008 United Methodist General Conference, April 22 - May 28 in Ft. Worth, Texas. Presenting the Young People’s Address to the 1,000 decision-makers of the United Methodist Church is a unique opporunity to make the voice of young people heard throughout the Church.

The Division on Ministries with Young People is now accepting submissions of videotapes from persons who would like to deliver the Young People’s Address. This opportunity is open any United Methodist young person who will be between 12-30 years of age at the time of General Conference. The deadline for this process is Oct. 1, 2006. Click MORE to find out how one of your youth or young adults can apply.
