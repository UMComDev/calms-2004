Affiliated organizations, while not part of the official United Methodist Church structure, provide support and resources to the mission and ministry of the denomination.

The following list of general boards and agencies of The United Methodist Church officially recognize and support affiliated organizations in various ways. The list of affiliated organizations are organized by sponsor agency.

  • General Board of Discipleship
  • General Council on Finance and Administration
  • General Board of Higher Education and Ministry
  • General Board of Global Ministries
  • General Commission on United Methodist Men
  • United Methodist Communications
  • General Board of Pension and Health Benefits
