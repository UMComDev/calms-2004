Submitted Petition Text:
Whereas, the Mission Society for United Methodists, an unofficial, independent missionary-sending organization founded and operated by United Methodists in 1984, is now celebrating its twentieth anniversary of work in missions and Christian discipleship;
Whereas, the Mission Society has provided innovative opportunities for allowing United Methodists with the missionary call to exercise their calling as United Methodists when the General Board of Global Ministries could not fit them within its structure;
Whereas, the Mission Society has taken extraordinary pains not to compete with the General Board of Global Ministries by pioneering missionary efforts only in regions where the Board is not working or where invited by our sister autonomous churches;
Whereas, the Mission Society has exercised responsible stewardship of its donations throughout its existence as a member of the Evangelical Council for Financial Accountability,
Therefore, be it resolved, that the General Conference congratulates the Mission Society for United Methodists on its twentieth anniversary and hopes for continued harmonious cooperation between the Mission Society, our congregations, our annual conferences, and the General Board of Global Ministries, and  
Be it further resolved, that as long as the Mission Society maintains these high standards of operation, consistent with our present and existing standards of doctrine, the General Conference urges bishops and annual conferences to support special appointments to serve in the Mission Society for United Methodists with the same enthusiasm as they show to missionaries serving the General Board of Global Ministries.
Submitted ByJonathan Carlsen
Chicago IL
