Join the CEF 2008 Conference Yahoo Group!

For early announcements about the conference join this online group.

  1. Send an entirely blank message (no subject line, no text) to
    cef2008-subscribe@yahoogroups.com.
  2. Sometime later in the day, you'll receive a message from yahoogroups.com asking you to confirm your subscription. Be sure to click on the link in that message!
  3. You're done! However, if your email program filters a lot of spam, you might need to add cef2008@yahoogroups.com to your "approved" list.
  4. Questions? Having trouble? Contact Tim Gossett, list moderator, at xnedwriter@mac.com.
