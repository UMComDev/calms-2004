The Volunteer Opportunity Board allows you to put local needs in front of an interested audience in your area. Your volunteer opportunity should be a specific activity within your local church community.

Your volunteer opportunity must have a designated event coordinator and be sponsored by an officially recognized United Methodist organization. All submissions are subject to verification and approval by UMC.org.

If you have questions or need to make revisions, please contact: volunteers@umc.org

If you want to submit a volunteer opportunity for United Methodist Volunteers in Mission, please contact your UMVIM Coordinator.
