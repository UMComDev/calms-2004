 About Us

Christian Educators Fellowship nurtures, supports, challenges and advocates for persons with responsibility for fostering discipleship through Christian education.
 Welcome From Our President
Dear Colleagues in Ministry,

It is a joy for me to welcome you to our new CEF website. Christian Educators Fellowship is a group of over 1,000 Christian educators (mostly serving United Methodist congregations or conference, college, seminary or church agencies) who are committed to nurture, support, advocate for and challenge the church through educational ministries. We welcome our sisters and brothers from pan-Methodist traditions into full membership as well.

CEF has been an important place for me since the late 1960's when I first attended a national CEF conference. We invite any Christian educators who are not yet members to join with us as we seek to live wholly and serve faithfully as disciples of Jesus Christ. CEF welcomes all who choose to work and serve with us.

Serving joyfully,

Linda Vogel
CEF President
