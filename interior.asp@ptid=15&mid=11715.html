2006 CEF Conference held October 5-9, at Lake Junaluska, NC

A Story, A Memory, a Journey

This Conference in that beautiful setting is over, but the memories linger from a wonderful (one of the best, some said!) time of learning, worship, and fellowship.  705 persons attended various events from pre-conference seminars, special concerts, a mountain spiritual pilgrimage, provocative Bible study, 125 workshop options, daily tours into the surrounding mountains, films, a packed resource center, coffee houses, plus good food and hospitality. 

The theme, “Loving the Sojourner through Teaching and Learning” wove together the days as Christian educators learned new skills and insights for leading their local churches to learn and grow in faithful Christian discipleship.  Sometimes they were challenged to ask, “Who are the wanderers in our midst? For what and whom are they searching?  Other times, they realized that they were the stranger being welcomed to the banquet table.  It all came together in their sense of renewal and affirmation as Christian educators, and for their shared journey in CEF.
